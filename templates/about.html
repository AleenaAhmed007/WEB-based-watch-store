Our Story

Crafting timeless elegance since 1985

Est. 1985

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985 by master horologist Jonathan Haven, Watch Haven began as a small workshop dedicated to the meticulous craft of watchmaking. What started as a passionate pursuit of perfection has grown into a globally recognized brand synonymous with luxury, precision, and timeless elegance.

For over three decades, we've remained true to our founding principles: exceptional craftsmanship, innovative design, and uncompromising quality. Every Watch Haven timepiece represents a harmonious blend of traditional watchmaking techniques and cutting-edge technology.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day to create exceptional timepieces

Excellence

We strive for perfection in every detail, from the selection of materials to the final polish of each timepiece. Every watch that bears our name undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

Innovation

While honoring traditional watchmaking techniques, we continuously push the boundaries of innovation. Our design team works tirelessly to incorporate new technologies and materials that enhance both form and function.

Heritage

We honor the rich legacy of watchmaking while creating timepieces that will be treasured for generations. A Watch Haven timepiece is not merely an accessory but an heirloom to be passed down through the years.
